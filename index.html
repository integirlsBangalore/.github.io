INTEGIRLS Bangalore

Our Mission

We work to create a safe, empowering space for girls and non-binary students in India to explore mathematics confidently. We recognize how gender bias and systemic barriers affect access to STEM for many students, especially Non Binary youth. Our goal is to support young learners early—helping them discover joy in problem solving, grow into competitive thinkers, explore STEM pathways, and gain access to opportunities including olympiads, mentorship, and international programs.

Upcoming Events

INTEGIRLS Bangalore will host two free math competitions in the 2025–2026 school year: one in fall and one in spring. There will be **three divisions: 6–8, 9–10, and 11–12**. Top participants will earn exciting awards and may be selected for our **Math Camp led by IMO/EGMO medalists and campers**. All girls, female-identifying, and non-binary teens across India may participate—no experience required! Sign-ups for the fall competition will open soon.

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

I’m interested but have no math competition experience. Can I still attend?


Of course, you should really attend this competition. In the past, many people have stated that INTEGIRLS gave them an entry into math competitions and they had no idea how it was until they attended the event. If you would like any pointers to understand how to prepare, you can feel free to email us. Hope to see you at our event(s).

I do not have a INTEGIRLS Chapter near my area, but I am interested in participating in the competition. Can I still attend?


We also encourage students not only from the Bangalore/India to attend, but also other areas as well. You may reach out to us at the links provided at the bottom of this page.

Who can I contact if I have any questions?


You can contact us through Email or Instagram at the links provided at the bottom of this page. It may take up to 2 weeks to get a reply from us through email. If it is urgent, you can message us on Instagram or add "URGENT" is subject line of your email.

I do not identify as female, but I would like to participate. How can I do that?


If you do not identify as female or non-binary, we encourage you to check out other opportunities we may share. You can contact bangalore@integirls.org to find more activities apt for you.

How can I contribute?


You may choose to volunteer for us by emailing us at [Bangalore]@integirls.org or can donate to our non-profit here. You can also email us at bangalore@integirls.org to receive more information about how you can help. YOUR HELP MAKES A DIFFERENCE TO US.

How many competitions will you conduct throughout the year?


We are targeting to host 2 competitions a year: one in the fall and one in the spring. More details about these competitions will be available on this website soon.
